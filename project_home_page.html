Our Goals

As high school students, we understand how how stressfull it may be trying to find scholarships for college. The process for each one can be long and tricky, and often students do not keep track of all of them and can not reach their fullest potential


Here are GYST (Get Your Scholarships Together), we hope to create an website all high school students use for their scholarship application process. We also hope to create an environment where students can learn about the various scholarships that are offered. More often than not, students get anxious and weary of whether they'll be able to cover the costs of a college education, or there may be students from underpriviledged background who can't seek guidance from those around them. Whatever kind of student you may be, this website will allow you to share your experiences with others, and grow.
